# Revenue Concentration Analysis & Business Intelligence Dashboard

## Project Overview

Built an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution to analyze customer revenue concentration, sales performance, profitability trends, and business risk using SQL, Python, and Power BI.

The project identifies high-revenue customers, revenue dependency risks, product performance, salesperson performance, and Year-over-Year business growth through interactive dashboards.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze revenue concentration across customers
- Identify customer dependency risks using Pareto analysis
- Monitor Year-over-Year sales growth
- Evaluate product profitability
- Analyze salesperson performance
- Build interactive dashboards for business decision-making

---
